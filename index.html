FIO Addresses

A FIO Address is a single identifier that eliminates the need to see, or even know about, blockchain public addresses. It's simple to register, easy to use, and works with every token or coin automatically.

Don't just Send. Request.

FIO Requests

Stop asking people what you owe them - let them tell you. Enjoy in-app requests for funds, done in an encrypted and private manner. Available for every crypto.
